What are signs that your dog is getting older?

  1. The weight of your dog is either getting bigger or smaller
  2. When your dog fails to respond to you
  3. When your sleeps more than usual
  4. When your dog slows down like walking up or down a hill
  5. If your dog experiences constipation
  6. When he or she has joint pain or stiffness
  7. Should you find her bumping into objects and loss of sense
